How to use Beat Lab

Use the big round button on the front of the pedestal to start and stop Beat Lab.

Click anywhere on the dial to add an instrument trigger.

After a trigger has been added, you can change its pitch with the up or down arrow.

Click an existing trigger to remove it.

Changes may not seem to take effect until the next time the trigger plays.

Mod buttons

Mods can click the "mods only" button so that only moderators can add or remove triggers.

Mods can click the padlock icon to allow anyone in the room, not just mods, to use the 3 buttons below it.

Click the reload button to load the next template.

Click the clear button to remove all the triggers so that you can start from a clean slate.

Click the double-up button to double the amount of beats on the dial, and duplicate all the triggers.
